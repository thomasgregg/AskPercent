AskPercent Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

AskPercent is designed to work locally on your device. We do not run a backend service for app calculations.

Data Collection

  • We do not collect personal data from inside the app.
  • Queries, history, favorites, and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not sell your data.

Third-Party Services

  • No analytics SDK is required for core functionality.
  • When you visit GitHub links, GitHub's own policies apply.

Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue at github.com/thomasgregg/AskPercent/issues.
