Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

AskPercent is designed for local use. Percentage parsing and calculations run on your device.

Data Collection

  • We do not collect personal data from app usage.
  • Queries, history, favorites, and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not sell your data.

Third-Party Services

  • No analytics SDK is required for core calculations.
  • If you visit GitHub links, GitHub policies apply there.

Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue at github.com/thomasgregg/AskPercent/issues.
